# Execution Flow

## Objective

The execution layer is designed to maintain operational stability under live market conditions.

---

## Core Flow

Signal Detection
↓
Execution Validation
↓
Risk Permission Layer
↓
Broker Execution
↓
Position Monitoring
↓
Exposure Control

---

## Execution Validation

The validation layer filters unstable conditions before execution.

Focus areas:
- spread behaviour
- volatility spikes
- execution quality
- exposure conditions

---

## Risk Permission Layer

Risk checks are applied before execution approval.

Examples:
- exposure limits
- drawdown state
- market instability
- recovery isolation

---

## Operational Focus

The infrastructure prioritizes:
- execution consistency
- stability
- controlled exposure
- live market adaptability
