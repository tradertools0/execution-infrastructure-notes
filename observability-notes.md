# Observability Notes

## Objective

The observability infrastructure is designed to maintain operational visibility and execution-state awareness during live market conditions.

The objective is continuous infrastructure monitoring, operational diagnostics and execution transparency under changing execution environments.

---

## Core Observability Structure

Execution Activity
        ↓
State Monitoring Layer
        ↓
Operational Logging
        ↓
Exposure Observation
        ↓
Execution Diagnostics
        ↓
Anomaly Detection
        ↓
Protection Escalation Awareness
        ↓
Operational Evaluation

---

## Execution-State Visibility

The infrastructure continuously monitors execution-state conditions during active operational phases.

Observed execution-state information may include:
- execution transitions
- broker response conditions
- order-state progression
- execution acknowledgement behaviour
- exposure-state movement

The objective is stable operational visibility during live execution handling.

---

## Operational Logging

The infrastructure maintains structured operational logs for execution observation and infrastructure analysis.

Operational logging may include:
- execution events
- exposure transitions
- recovery activity
- validation-layer behaviour
- protection-layer escalation
- execution restriction activity

The objective is consistent operational traceability during changing execution environments.

---

## Exposure Observation

Active exposure conditions are continuously evaluated throughout live operational activity.

Observed exposure conditions may include:
- directional concentration
- execution density
- exposure escalation
- recovery-state behaviour
- operational instability conditions

The infrastructure prioritizes continuous exposure awareness before allowing additional execution participation.

---

## Execution Diagnostics

Execution diagnostics help identify unstable operational behaviour during live execution phases.

Diagnostic observation areas may include:
- execution delays
- unstable fills
- spread instability
- synchronization inconsistency
- degraded execution response

The objective is early operational instability detection before exposure conditions deteriorate further.

---

## Anomaly Detection

The infrastructure continuously evaluates for abnormal operational behaviour and unexpected execution conditions.

Observed anomalies may include:
- abnormal spread expansion
- execution clustering
- inconsistent execution-state transitions
- directional instability
- broker-side execution irregularities

Protective handling behaviour may include:
- execution throttling
- operational restriction escalation
- exposure limitation
- temporary execution suppression

The objective is controlled operational behaviour during unstable execution environments.

---

## Protection Escalation Awareness

Protective infrastructure behaviour adapts according to changing operational conditions.

Protection-state observation may include:
- execution restriction escalation
- recovery isolation activation
- exposure reduction behaviour
- operational degradation handling
- protection-layer transitions

The infrastructure continuously evaluates protection-state effectiveness during volatile market phases.

---

## Operational Evaluation

Observability infrastructure may be used to evaluate:
- execution consistency
- operational stability
- exposure survivability
- recovery behaviour
- infrastructure adaptability
- protection-layer effectiveness

The objective is continuous operational awareness during live infrastructure activity.

---

## Operational Philosophy

The objective of observability is not passive monitoring.

The objective is active operational awareness, infrastructure visibility and execution-state understanding during live market uncertainty.

The infrastructure prioritizes:
- operational transparency
- execution awareness
- infrastructure visibility
- adaptive monitoring
- long-term operational resilience
